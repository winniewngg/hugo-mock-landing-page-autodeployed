Amazon Review Scraping🛡️

AmazonExplorer automatically pulls reviews from any Amazon product page, filtering out low-quality noise so you can quickly see what real customers are saying. Get fast insights without digging through hundreds of comments.

Ebay Review Scraping🚀

Our newest feature brings structured eBay reviews directly into your browser. Compare experiences across platforms and uncover product patterns before making a purchase—no more guessing based on seller ratings alone.

ChatGPT Integration📈

With one click, send scraped reviews to ChatGPT for intelligent summarization and sentiment analysis. Instantly understand the big picture—what people like, what they hate, and whether it’s worth buying.
